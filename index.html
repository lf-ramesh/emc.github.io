SCHOOLS CLEANING SERVICES

Keeping schools clean is an important responsibility. EMC Services provides the highest level of service in educational institutions, meeting or surpassing all the standards needed to ensure the health, safety, and attractive environment for students. Educational institutions are the pride of a community, and EMC is dedicated to serving them. This includes private and public schools, day cares, college/university campuses, and student residences / hostels.

Educational buildings require careful, constant, and detailed attention. They are not only busy, high-traffic areas, but the variety of individuals – students, teachers, administrators, and visitors – have a broad range of needs. EMC meets these challenges with professionalism, care, and flexibility to the ever-changing requirements of schools.

EMC Services provides a wide range of services for schools:

  • High level cleaning, with attention to infection control and touch point cleaning.
  • After-school events can be staffed, even for large gatherings.
  • Sports events require special cleaning, before and after.
  • Even when school is out, we’re still working. Vacation times provide the opportunity to work on full cleaning programmes and special projects which cannot be done while school is in session.
  • Community events and other third-party use of schools can be covered by EMC staff.
  • Day, evening, weekend, monthly coverage.

Infection control and the use of non-toxic, environmentally safe cleaning materials are high-priority concerns for EMC Services. To ensure the highest level of safety, all EMC Services cleaning team members assigned to primary and secondary schools are screened and cleared for vulnerable sector. All back-up personnel assigned to augment staff or as substitutes for regularly scheduled staff is also screened and cleared for vulnerable sector.

All of our cleaning team members are fully bonded and supervised by EMC Services management personnel.

HOSPITALS CLEANING SERVICES

In EMC Services these facilities, including clinics, hospitals and Old age home, require particularly rigorous cleaning care and treatment. Infection control, safety, and a fresh, pleasant environment are all important considerations. EMC Services maintaining the highest level of cleanliness in healthcare operations.

All procedures, techniques, equipment and cleaning/disinfectant products are selected and maintained to provide the cleanest and safest environment for patients, staff, and visitors.

These best practices include:

  • Use of only approved equipment, products and procedures
  • Training and continued education of all EMC staff in proper procedures
  • Setting and maintaining appropriate cleaning schedules
  • Continued audits and measurements of cleaning and disinfection
  • Close consultation and communication with all government, health and administrative entities to ensure a continued high level of cleaning

Since these facilities combine both the challenges of high-traffic buildings and areas at high risk for cross-infection, the cleaning and maintenance model adhered to by EMC Services is designed to provide strict standards of cleanliness and safety, while still providing a comfortable environment.

Practices include:

  • Frequent touch point disinfection
  • Segregation of equipment in building zones to prevent cross-contamination
  • Appropriate handling of bedding and other durable materials
  • The use of noise-reduced equipment

The EMC Services cleaning staff assigned to these facilities are specially trained to understand and follow all procedures and requirements for this important work. All of our cleaning team members are fully bonded and supervised by EMC Services management personnel.

INDUSTRIAL CLEANING SERVICES

Industrial buildings, particularly ones that include manufacturing facilities, offer special needs and challenges for their cleaning and maintenance requirements. EMC Services offers services tailored to the needs of industrial building occupants to maintain a clean and healthy workplace. A safety issue, a paramount concern in the industrial buildings, is also addressed through EMC Services’ cleaning programs.

EMC Services staff will meet with facilities staff to determine specific requirements for your building and will work with you to plan a cleaning program and schedule that meets your needs. Depending upon the materials used or present in your building, work floor layout and company schedule, we can provide special material handling needs, choices of cleaning processes, equipment and cleaning products which work best for you.

Services for industrial buildings include scheduled/routine and special cleaning of:

  • Entrance, common, and public areas
  • Equipment and workstation areas
  • Office areas
  • Warehousing areas
  • Kitchen, break room, and other food handling/preparation areas
  • Washroom, locker room and showering facilities
  • Hard floors

In addition, Sunshine can offer:

  • Power washing of concrete floors, building exteriors, sidewalks, loading areas, private roads, and parking lots
  • On-site trash and recycling handling
  • Preparing for visitor tours (cleaning sites for visiting executives, owners, investors and other visitors)

All of our cleaning team members are fully bonded and supervised by EMC Services management personnel.

OFFICE & SHOW ROOMS CLEANING SERVICES

A clean, pleasant and healthy environment is an important consideration for office workers when determining their job satisfaction. Your staff deserves the best conditions that can be provided, and EMC Services offers office cleaning services dedicated to satisfying your needs. We will work with you to evaluate your requirements and to customize our cleaning program to the specific concerns of your company or organization. Office janitorial services that EMC Services provide:

  • Scheduling cleaning to your needs
  • Recycling and on-site sorting according to your company’s policy
  • Special event cleaning for meetings, presentations and conferences
  • Use of noise-reduced equipment to minimize distraction
  • Use of dry vacuum cleaners to reduce dust levels
  • Touch point cleaning, including washroom and kitchen/break room facilities
  • Colour coded micro fibre cloths to eliminate cross contamination
  • Porter/matron services to provide continuous cleaning, freshening and restocking of washroom paper products
  • Scheduled cleaning of entrance and public areas to maintain appearance
  • Scheduled hard floor/carpeting and upholstery cleaning

All of our cleaning equipment, procedures and products are selected to provide services that are both effective and safe for your office environment. The level of cleaning we provide not only provides a safe and attractive workplace, but provides a healthier environment for your staff and visitors. Maintaining a high level of cleanliness also extends the lifetime of your floors and furniture, reducing long-term costs for your company or organization.

Our well-trained, uniformed staff undergoes a continuous process of training and evaluation to maintain high standards of cleaning and maintenance. With over 825 full and part-time cleaning staff, we can provide uninterrupted staffing for your office. EMC Services maintains an enhanced supervisor-to-cleaner ratio to guarantee the delivery of service suitable to your needs.

All of our cleaning team members are fully bonded and supervised by EMC Services management personnel.

COMMERCIAL& RETAILS BUILDING CLEANING SERVICES

Commercial buildings depend upon a clean, inviting appearance to attract visitors and customers. It is extremely important that entrance areas, lobbies, common areas, waiting areas and washrooms be kept spotless to uphold the public perception of your business. EMC Services understands the importance of this and works hard to help your company present a clean, attractive presence. Whether you are located in a multi-tenant building or have a stand-alone unit, EMC Services is prepared to help you look your best.

EMC’s team of well-trained, dedicated cleaning staff is ready and able to clean and maintain your company’s public areas, staff offices, behind-the-scenes work spaces, and storage areas. We take care of the cleaning so you can concentrate on your most important job: serving your customers.

Our services include:

  • Cleaning entrances, lobbies, common areas, staircases and elevators
  • Cleaning and refreshing washrooms
  • Window cleaning
  • Power washing of non-municipal parking areas, sidewalks, and paths
  • Touch point cleaning in areas where dirt and infection transfer risks are highest
  • Hard floor cleaning and maintenance
  • Carpeting and upholstery cleaning and maintenance
  • Behind-the-scenes area cleaning (including offices, work areas, break rooms and storage areas)
  • Special event cleaning for grand openings, sales events, and conferences, etc.
